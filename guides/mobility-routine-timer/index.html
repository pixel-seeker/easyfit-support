Make mobility easy to start

Mobility work often gets skipped because it feels unstructured. A timer gives it a clear beginning, middle, and end, which makes it easier to repeat.

Use short positions or flows

You can time each position, alternate sides, or build a simple flow with transitions. The right setup depends on whether the session is a warm-up, cooldown, recovery block, or movement break.

Keep cues simple

For mobility, the timer should be calm and predictable. Clear transitions are more useful than aggressive alerts, especially when the goal is control and consistency.

How EasyFit helps

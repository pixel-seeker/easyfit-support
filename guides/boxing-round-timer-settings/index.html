Boxing round timer settings that are easy to follow.

Set up boxing rounds, rest periods, alerts, warm-up, cooldown, and haptic cues for boxing, martial arts, and conditioning workouts.

Use round-based timing

Boxing workouts are easier to follow when the timer is built around rounds and recovery. Pick the round length, rest length, number of rounds, and whether you need a preparation countdown before the first bell.

Make alerts obvious

A boxing timer should be readable and audible without interrupting the session. Sound, haptics, and voice cues help you know when a round starts, when rest begins, and when the final seconds are coming.

Adapt the format

The same round timer can work for shadow boxing, heavy bag rounds, jump rope, martial arts drills, and conditioning. Adjust the number of rounds and rest periods to match the session.

How EasyFit helps

EasyFit supports round timers with custom work, rest, preparation, cooldown, cues, Apple Watch workflows, music controls, and workout history.

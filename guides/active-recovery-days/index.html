Active recovery days with timed movement.

Use active recovery days for lighter timed movement, mobility, stretching, cooldowns, and habit-building between harder workouts.

Recovery does not have to mean doing nothing

Some days are better suited for lighter movement than high intensity work. A short timer can help you keep recovery sessions focused and avoid turning them into another hard workout.

Use gentle blocks

Timed mobility, stretching, breathing, walking breaks, or easy bodyweight movement can create structure without requiring a complex plan. Keep cues calm and durations realistic.

Protect the habit

Active recovery days can keep the training habit alive while still respecting fatigue. The goal is to finish feeling more organized, not exhausted.

How EasyFit helps

EasyFit supports mobility timers, cooldown blocks, gentle intervals, exercise lists, haptics, widgets, Apple Watch workflows, and progress tracking.

This guide is general training information, not medical advice. Adjust workouts to your level and follow professional guidance when needed.

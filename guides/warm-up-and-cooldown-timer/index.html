Why preparation time matters

Preparation time gives you a clean start. It is useful when you need to put the phone down, adjust music, move to the first exercise, or start an Apple Watch workout flow.

Using warm-up blocks

Warm-up blocks can be timed like any other interval. Keeping them in the same timer helps the whole session feel planned instead of improvised.

Using cooldown blocks

Cooldown timing gives the session a clear ending and can be useful for mobility, stretching, breathing, or simply logging the workout consistently.

How EasyFit helps

EasyFit supports preparation, warm-up-style intervals, cooldown, exercise lists, haptics, voice cues, Apple Watch context, and workout history.

This guide is general training information, not medical advice. Adjust workouts to your level and follow professional guidance when needed.

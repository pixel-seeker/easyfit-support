Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

EasyFit ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

Data Collection

Our apps are designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

Data Storage

All app data (such as workout settings and history) is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@easyfit.support.

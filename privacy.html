Privacy Notice

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT — Last updated: June 11, 2026

This notice explains how Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT handles information when you use the iPhone app, widgets, and Apple Watch companion experience. The app is provided by Sergei Melchugov ("EasyFit", "we", "us"). The app is designed so most training data stays on your own devices or in Apple services you control.

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT does not sell your personal information. HealthKit data is not used for advertising and is not sent to third-party ad networks.

2. Data handled by the app

Health and activity data

With your permission, the app may read or write Apple Health data such as heart rate, active energy, workout records, date of birth, body mass, and height. This information is used to show live metrics, estimate energy, and save completed sessions to Apple Health.

Health, workout, heart-rate, calorie, weight, and plan information in the app is provided for general fitness and wellness use only. It is not medical advice and should not be used for diagnosis, treatment, emergency decisions, rehabilitation, or clinical monitoring.

Onboarding and fitness preferences

The app may store information you enter during onboarding or in settings, such as goals, target weight, protected areas, workout preferences, coach choice, timer settings, reminders, and customization choices. This information is used to personalize the app experience on your device.

Apple Watch companion data

The Apple Watch app may collect heart-rate samples during an active session and exchange live session state with your iPhone through Watch Connectivity. Session status, haptic preferences, and display settings may be exchanged between your paired devices.

Session history and routines

Completed sessions, custom routines, milestones, preferences, and widget summaries are stored locally with Apple platform storage technologies such as SwiftData and UserDefaults. If iCloud sync is enabled, compatible non-health app data may be stored in your private iCloud container. HealthKit data, body measurements, heart-rate summaries, heart-rate zones, calorie estimates, and weight values are not synced to iCloud by EasyFit.

Analytics and diagnostics

If analytics are allowed, the app may collect usage events such as screen views, feature usage, subscription flow events, permission status, app instance or device identifiers, device type, app version, and operating system version. Crash, performance, and diagnostic reports may include technical logs, stack traces, app state, launch or hang information, and other technical data needed to fix reliability issues.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. RevenueCat may be used to validate receipts, manage entitlements, and show current product offerings. The app does not receive your credit card number or App Store payment details.

Device identifiers and attribution

If you grant App Tracking Transparency permission where requested, device-level identifiers may be used to measure app installs, subscription performance, and analytics reliability. These identifiers are not used to show third-party ads in the app or to sell personal information.

Music access

If you grant Apple Music permission, Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT can display or control playback during sessions. The app does not build a listening profile or sell music-library information.

Notifications

The app may schedule local reminders and Watch alerts if you allow notifications. You can turn these off in system settings or inside the app.

Support messages

If you contact support, the details you choose to send, such as name, device context, app version, issue category, message content, screenshots, or videos, are used only to respond to your request and improve support quality.

3. How data is used

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT uses data to:

  • run interval sessions and companion Watch features;
  • calculate session summaries, heart-rate zones, progress views, and energy estimates;
  • personalize onboarding, plans, routines, coach prompts, and settings;
  • save activity to Apple Health when permitted;
  • sync compatible non-health app data through iCloud if enabled;
  • manage subscriptions and premium access;
  • improve stability and diagnose crashes;
  • understand aggregate feature usage when analytics are allowed;
  • provide reminders and support responses.

4. Legal bases

  • contract, to provide app functionality, purchases, subscriptions, and support;
  • consent, for HealthKit, Apple Music, notifications, analytics where required, and other optional permissions;
  • legitimate interests, to keep the app reliable, secure, and understandable in aggregate;
  • legal obligations, where records or responses are required by law.

5. Storage and security

Most app data is stored on your device. iCloud data is stored in your Apple account infrastructure. Third-party service data is processed by the providers listed below under their own security practices.

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT may integrate with:

  • Apple HealthKit, iCloud, Apple Music, Watch Connectivity, WidgetKit, notifications, and App Store purchases;

These providers may process technical, purchase, analytics, crash, or platform data as needed to provide their services. Payment card details are handled by Apple and are not received by EasyFit.

7. International transfers

Data may be processed in the country where you use the app and in other countries where Apple, Google/Firebase, RevenueCat, or support providers operate. Where required, transfers rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or provider compliance programs.

8. Advertising and tracking

The app does not currently show third-party ads. App Store privacy labels may disclose device identifiers as used for tracking when the app requests App Tracking Transparency permission for install attribution and measurement. HealthKit data is not used for advertising, sold, or shared with ad networks. Health, fitness, purchase history, crash, performance, and diagnostic data are not used for tracking or shared with data brokers. If advertising features are added in the future, this notice will be updated before those features are used.

9. Children

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support so it can be reviewed and deleted where applicable.

10. Retention

Local routines, preferences, and session history remain until you delete them, reset the app, disable sync, or uninstall the app. Analytics, crash, purchase, and support data are retained according to the applicable provider settings and support needs.

11. Your choices

You can:

  • revoke Apple Health, Music, notification, and other permissions in system settings;
  • delete local app data by removing the app or clearing app data;
  • manage iCloud data through Apple settings;
  • manage subscriptions through your Apple ID account;
  • contact support for privacy questions.

12. EU/EEA, UK, and other privacy rights

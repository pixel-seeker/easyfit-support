A simple interval timer for structured training.

Interval Timer Tabata and HIIT is built for people who need a clear workout timer without noisy setup. Plan work and rest blocks, add exercises, follow voice and haptic cues, and keep sessions moving from warm-up to cooldown.

Use it as a Tabata timer, HIIT timer, circuit training timer, boxing round timer, EMOM timer, AMRAP timer, or a flexible home workout timer. Apple Watch, Apple Health, widgets, music controls, and progress views help keep training connected across your devices.

  • Tabata timer for classic 20/10 and custom intervals
  • HIIT interval timer with work, rest, rounds, and cooldown
  • Circuit, EMOM, AMRAP, boxing, and mobility sessions
  • Apple Watch heart rate, Apple Health, widgets, and haptics

Frequently asked questions.

What workouts can I time with the app?

You can create timed Tabata, HIIT, circuit training, EMOM, AMRAP, boxing, stretching, mobility, and home workout sessions with custom work, rest, rounds, preparation, cooldown, and exercise lists.

Does it work with Apple Watch and Apple Health?

Yes. The app supports Apple Watch heart-rate workflows and Apple Health integration when you grant permission, so workouts and health metrics can stay connected to your Apple fitness data.

Can I use the timer without watching the screen?

Voice prompts, haptic cues, session sounds, widgets, and music controls help you follow interval rounds without constantly looking at your iPhone.
